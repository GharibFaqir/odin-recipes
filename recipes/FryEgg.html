How to Fry an Egg?

Description:

Eggs are an amazing source of protein.

Today we will learn how to fry an egg.

Ingredients || Tools:

  • Stove
  • Pan (Non-stick Rocks)
  • Olive Oil || Butter || Vegetable Oil
  • An Egg

Steps:

  1. Light up the stove
  2. Place Pan on stove and glaze pan with oil/butter.
  3. After 30 seconds hover your hand over pan, you should feel the heat.
  4. It's time, crack the egg and pour on pan.
  5. Fry egg for 30 minutes on high flame.
  6. Transfer egg from stove to plate.
  7. Sprinkle salt and serve.
