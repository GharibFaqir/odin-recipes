How to make Instant Noodles (Japan)?

Description:

Those who love anime, love noodles!!

Ingredients || Tools:

  • Stove
  • Pot
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 packs of Instant Noodles

Steps:

  1. Turn on the stove.
  2. Place two cups of water in the pot.
  3. Place the put on stove.
  4. Bring the water in pot to boil.
  5. Open both packs of Instant Noodles
  6. Transfer Noodle cakes into the pot.
  7. Stir for a minute, then add noodle mix.
  8. Cook for another minute or two.
  9. Transfer cooked noodles from pot to bowl.
  10. Serve with either Boiled Egg, Fried Egg. Enjoy!!
