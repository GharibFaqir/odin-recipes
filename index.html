How to boil an egg?


Description:

Eggs are an amazing source of protein.

Today we will learn how to hard-boil and/or soft boil an egg.

Ingredients || Tools:

  • A Stove
  • A Pot
  • An Egg
  • Water (2 cups)

Steps:

  1. Put 2 cups of water and egg in the pot.
  2. Place pot on stove and turn on the stove on high.
  3. For a soft boiled Egg: Turn off the flame after 5 minutes.
  4. For a hard boiled Egg: Turn off the stove after 10 minutes.
  5. Drain the warm water immediately and run cold water until egg are cooled.
  6. Crack the shell, Serve with a glass of milk. Enjoy!
